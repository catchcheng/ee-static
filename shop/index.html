Curb-Side & In-Store Pick-Up

During this time, the safety of our customers comes first! With a priority on home cooking, we want to support our community who still want to feed their families quality, local products.

The store is open, but we encourage you to pre-order by either
1.) fill out the Order Request Form
2.) message us via Facebook / Instagram
3.) call us at 204-222-4888
Our staff will confirm the order details with you (contact name, number, and pick up time slot). Curbside pick up requires payment over the phone.
You can pick up the order either
1.) in store after making the payment
2.) curbside after calling us, we will bring your package out to your vehicle

**Please note that due to high volumes, orders could take up to 3 days to fill as we replenish our inventory. Thank you for your patience.
